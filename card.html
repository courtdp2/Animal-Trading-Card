Grey Wolf

Wolves have unique howls, like fingerprints, that scientists (and other pack members) can use to tell them apart.

  • Height: 2.6 – 2.8 ft. (At Shoulder)
  • Lifespan: 5 – 6 years
  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Mass: Male: 66 – 180 lbs, Female: 51 – 120 lbs

The gray wolf is a canine native to the wilderness and remote areas of Eurasia and North America. It is the largest extant member of its family, with males averaging 43–45 kg and females 36–38.5 kg.
